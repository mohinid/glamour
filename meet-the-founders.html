Meet The Founder

Abhishek Somani

Abhishek Somani, born in Jaipur to a humble family, started working in the jewelry business at the age of 15. Passionate about gemstones, designer jewelry, and uncompromising quality, he quickly climbed the ranks and became the Sales Head for Malaysia in 1998, driven by positive customer feedback. Seeking bigger opportunities, he moved to the US to serve a larger clientele.

Abhishek is an MBA, specializing in International Trade, with deep knowledge of gemstones and expertise in unique designing and manufacturing. His strategic vision and leadership have been instrumental in achieving global expansion for the enterprise. With a strong focus on innovation and quality, he has successfully positioned the brand as a trusted name in the international jewelry market, while continuing to explore new horizons in business growth and development in the Gulf region and beyond.

Married to Anjana Somani, she now helps with retail sales and client relationships, while Abhishek oversees export trades, manufacturing, and business expansion. Together, they form a dedicated team focused on the success of their jewelry enterprise.

Meet Anjana Somani | Co-Founder

Anjana Somani, co-founder of Glamour Art and Jewerly and wife of Abhishek Somani, will be leading our Dubai store Glam Artier. She brings a remarkable blend of expertise and experience to the table. With a background as a Lawyer, MBA in Human Resources and Marketing, Professor, Author, and media Representative, she has immense exposure to various industries. Since 2009, Anjana has accumulated over 15 years of experience in jewelry retail, sales, marketing, and public relations, playing a pivotal role in crafting stunning collections and building customer relationships. Her leadership at Glam Artier reflects a deep commitment to elegance and artistry in jewelry design.

Meet Himanshu Nagpal

Our Dubai store Glam Artier Jewelry's operations will be managed by Himanshu Nagpal, a seasoned Chartered Accountant with a strong background in Financial and Strategic Planning and Execution. He is also our childhood friend who holds a deep passion for jewelry and customer satisfaction. Himanshu has been residing in Dubai since 2012, and deeply understand the culture, traditions and people. As a driving force behind Glam Artier, Himanshu will work closely with Anjana to realize their ambition and vision of a global brand and expand further in Gulf Countries in the coming years..
